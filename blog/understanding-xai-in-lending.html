Understanding Explainable AI (XAI) in Lending

Published on June 5, 2025 by Stratagem AI Team

The adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the lending sector promises unprecedented efficiency and accuracy. However, this technological advancement brings forth a critical challenge: the "black box" problem...

Why is XAI Crucial?

For financial institutions, especially community banks and credit unions, XAI is not just a buzzword but a necessity. Here's why:

  • Regulatory Compliance: Regulations like ECOA and FCRA demand fairness and transparency. XAI helps demonstrate that AI models are not making discriminatory decisions.
  • Risk Management: Understanding why a model makes a certain prediction allows institutions to better assess and mitigate risks.
  • Building Trust: Transparency in decision-making builds trust with both customers and regulators.
